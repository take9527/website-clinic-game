Ethical Design Principles

  • Transparency
    Show costs, consequences, and choices clearly.
  • User Control
    Let users actively choose instead of relying on manipulative defaults.
  • Neutral Language
    Avoid guilt, shame, fear, or pressure in interface copy.
  • Balanced Choices
    Make accept and decline options equally visible and accessible.
  • Honest Urgency
    Use urgency only when it is accurate, verifiable, and relevant.
